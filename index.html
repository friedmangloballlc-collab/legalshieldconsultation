Frequently Asked Questions
Got Questions? We've Got Answers.
How much does LegalShield cost?▾
Personal plans start at $35.95/month — just over $1 per day. Small Business plans start at $49/month. Both include access to a dedicated law firm with no hourly fees or retainers.
Does LegalShield cover my whole family?▾
Yes! The Personal Plan covers you, your spouse, and your dependents — all under the same flat monthly rate. Every member of your household can call your provider law firm for help.
Can I cancel my membership anytime?▾
Yes. LegalShield is a pre-paid monthly subscription and members can cancel at any time. Cancellations must be submitted in writing by emailing memberservices@legalshield.com. Annual memberships can be cancelled after the first 12 months.
What if I have a legal issue that started before joining?▾
Most pre-existing situations can receive a consultation and a discount from the provider law firm. Some exclusions apply — for example, civil actions or IRS notices received before your membership start date may not receive full coverage, but you'll still get legal advice and a 25% discount on further services.
How is LegalShield different from LegalZoom?▾
LegalZoom primarily offers document templates you fill out yourself. LegalShield connects you with a real human attorney in your state who provides actual legal advice, answers your questions, reviews your documents, and can represent you. It's the difference between a form and a lawyer.
Are these real lawyers or a call center?▾
Real law firms. LegalShield has a network of nearly 40 vetted provider law firms and over 900 attorneys across the US and Canada, with an average of 22 years of experience. You're assigned to a dedicated firm in your state — not a generic hotline.
