Call Script
Expand each step for word-for-word talking points. Use this during every call.
1
Opening & Introduction
▼
"Hi [Name], my name is [Your Name] and I'm calling as a representative with LegalShield. Do you have just a couple of minutes? I'd love to share something that could save you a lot of money and stress when it comes to legal matters."
💡
Keep it warm and conversational. Lead with the problem, not the price. Most people have dealt with a legal situation they didn't know how to handle.
2
Discovery — Find Their Pain Point
▼
"Quick question — have you or anyone in your family ever been in a situation where you needed legal help — a lease issue, a speeding ticket, a business contract, or you just didn't know your rights... but felt like you couldn't afford a lawyer?"
💡
Let them talk. Their pain point is your pitch angle. Common triggers: landlord disputes, traffic tickets, business contracts, estate planning.
3
Present the Solution
▼
"That's exactly what LegalShield fixes. For less than a dollar a day — starting at just $35.95 a month — you get access to a real law firm in your state with lawyers averaging 22 years of experience. No hourly fees. No retainers."
"It's like having a lawyer in the family. They'll review documents, write letters on your behalf, help with your will, deal with IRS issues — and if you ever get in a real legal situation, they've got your back."
💡
Key phrases: "Less than $1/day" — "Real law firm, not a call center" — "22 years average experience" — "Available 24/7"
4
Match to the Right Plan
▼
"Based on what you've shared, the [Plan Name] would be the perfect fit. For just $[XX] a month, you'd get [highlight 2–3 features relevant to their situation]."
💡
Match rules: Side hustle/freelancer → + Home Business. Rideshare driver → + Rideshare Add-On. Business owner → Small Business Essentials/Plus. Concerned about lawsuits → + Trial Defense.
5
Handle Hesitation & Close
▼
"I totally understand. But here's what I always tell people — legal issues don't wait for a convenient time. Most members say they got their money's worth the very first time they called. And you can cancel in writing any time."
"Can I get you set up today? It only takes a few minutes and you'll have access right away."
💡
Emphasize low risk: monthly subscription, cancel anytime. Remind them $200+/hr for one outside attorney call vs. this flat fee.
6
Wrap-Up & Next Steps
▼
"Great! I'll walk you through signing up — it's quick and easy. After enrolling, you'll get a welcome email with your membership number. Download the LegalShield app and your provider law firm's number will be right there."
💡
After the call: Immediately update their lead status in the portal and log your call notes while fresh. Set a follow-up reminder if needed.
FAQs & Objections
Click to expand. 🔴 = objection — use your response. ❓ = info question.
"I already have a lawyer." 🔴▼
Objection

That's great — but even people with attorneys use LegalShield for everyday questions they'd never call their lawyer about. When did you last call them for a lease review or traffic ticket? Most don't — because it's $200+/hr. LegalShield handles all of that for a flat monthly fee. You keep your attorney for the big stuff.

"It's too expensive." 🔴▼
Objection

$35.95/month is about the cost of dinner out. One call to an outside attorney costs $200–$400/hr. If you ever need legal help even once, LegalShield pays for itself many times over. Most members get their money's worth on the very first call.

"How is this different from LegalZoom?" 🔴▼
Objection

LegalZoom is mainly a document template service — you fill out forms yourself. LegalShield connects you with a real human attorney in your state who gives actual legal advice, answers your specific questions, and can represent you. It's the difference between a template and a lawyer who picks up the phone.

"Is this a real law firm or a call center?" ❓▼

Real law firms. LegalShield has nearly 40 vetted provider law firms and 900+ attorneys across the US and Canada, averaging 22 years of experience. You're assigned to a dedicated firm in your state — not a generic hotline.

"What about pre-existing legal issues?" ❓▼

Most pre-existing situations still get a consultation and a 25% discount from your provider firm. Civil actions or IRS notices received before membership start are excluded from full coverage, but you still save significantly vs. hiring a lawyer cold.

"Can I cancel anytime?" ❓▼

Yes — cancel anytime in writing. Email memberservices@legalshield.com with your name, membership number, and cancellation request. Annual memberships can cancel after the first 12 months.

"Does it cover my whole family?" ❓▼

Yes! The Personal Plan covers you, your spouse, and dependents — all under the same flat monthly rate. It's one of the biggest value advantages of the plan.

"I need to think about it." 🔴▼
Objection

Of course — I want you to feel comfortable. Just remember legal issues don't wait. The risk of signing up and not needing it is small. The risk of needing a lawyer without coverage can be very expensive. You can cancel in writing anytime. What specific concern can I answer right now?
